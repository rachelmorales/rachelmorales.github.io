Gender Gelatein

I casted gender symbols out of transparent gelatin as to not assign a color to a gender and to show that we are all made out of the same material, I also took this opportunity to inform people about all of the different genders that people might identify as, I understand that some people might find this topic intimidating or confusing so I left the symbols out in the sun where they would eventually melt away symbolizing that this topic is conquerable and shouldn't be feared.
