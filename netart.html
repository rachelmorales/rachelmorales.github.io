Net Art

Human Code
This is my second completed Net Art project called “Human Code”. Over the course of 2 weeks, I recorded my emotions at the end of everyday and how much I felt them on a scale of 1 to 10.I then assigned every emotion to a corresponding color in a circle shape and expanded the circle to the amount I felt that emotion and deflated it once it reached the maximum number recorded.
I was exploring using technology to learn more about myself and displaying it in an abstract way. Anyone can right click on the site and select inspect element to reveal the code behind the website. Here you can see what emotion is assigned to what color.
