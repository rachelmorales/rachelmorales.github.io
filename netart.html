Privacy Please
This is my finished Net Art project called “Privacy Please” is an interactive website of 8 pages that I hand coded that displays the empty shell of day to day internet browsing. Among the pages I address how we do not have any privacy on the internet and if we want to achieve that then we have to put more information out. The pages are looped to create an endless cycle of familiar motions and terms that eventually leads you nowhere. Once you log on you can’t truly log off. I installed it in a gallery around my school and completed it by making 4 by 4 cards with a 2d design logo of the project name and the url on the back.
