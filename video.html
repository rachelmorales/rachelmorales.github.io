What Hair Means To Me

Digital Video, 3:14 min

For this piece I projected a video onto the floor that shows hair that has been cut falling to the ground. Different women are heard narrating while the hair strands fall. They talk about what hair means to them, what value is held in it by today’s society, and what value, if any, do they hold in it personally. In many of the interviews the women explain what it is like to stray away from the typical social norms of hair that women are held to today, how it makes them feel, and how they got to that point.
